PHYSICIST NETWORK
Welcome to our physicist network project!

We used web scraping techniques to collect data from Wikipedia and analyzed the connections between physicists to understand how the field of physics has evolved over time. Through our research, we uncovered some fascinating insights about the relationships between physicists and how they have influenced each other's work.

WHY A PHYSICIST NETWORK

As a group of data scientists, we were interested in exploring the relationships between physicists and how ideas and concepts have spread throughout the field over time. To do this, we used the Wikipedia network to analyze the connections between different physicists and their influence on each other. The Wikipedia network allowed us to track the evolution of different research areas and topics, and explore the patterns of collaboration and communication between different physicists and research communities. By using network analysis techniques, we were able to identify the most important and influential physicists in the field and trace the influence of their work on other researchers.
